Welcome to Gifts After Sex. This show displays a capsule of clothing, which is part of a greater, on going, project. With a goal to utilize art as a driving force for change, Gas was created, an integrated platform for artists and thinkers worldwide to showcase their work, while providing streamlined collaboration between creatives.

Goal

We hope to cultivate cooperation between artists within the platform to orchestrate high-quality original projects; apparel, films, sculptures, etc, connecting creatives within all fields. GAS incorporates the same philosophy of utilizing artwork to facilitate creation and inspire change within the world, and applies it to fashion. This first collection, Principium, serves to assist artistic program, granting the wearer freedom to assemble supplies that fuel the creative process to their liking. Creating garments which are both elegant and functional.

What is this gift? Life* The most powerful creative statement in the universe. Using artwork to facilitate creation and inspire change within the world, GAS represents the fuel for the engine that is you and I.
