Welcome to GAS

About

Welcome to Gifts After Sex. This show displays a capsule of clothing, that is part of a greater collection, which is part of a greater, on going, years long project. Together with my close friend, Luca Ross, we partnered with a goal, to utilize art as a driving force for change. We developed and created GAS, an integrated platform for artists and thinkers worldwide to showcase their work, while providing streamlined collaboration between creatives.

Food

Whether you're vegan, vegetarian, pescetarian, flexitarian, gluten-free, lactose intolerant, sugar-free or you eat just about anything, we have culinary classes for you! Specialist diet or not.
